# code of conduct

be respectful. critique code, not people. harassment, hate speech, dogwhistles, outing, and doxxing aren't welcome here. if you wouldn't say it to a coworker, don't say it in the repo.

## scope

the github repo (issues, PRs, discussions, reviews), and anywhere you're acting as a capscr contributor.

## reporting

email the maintainer listed in `Cargo.toml` with links or screenshots if they help. reports stay confidential.

the maintainer acknowledges within a week, looks into it, and picks one of: a warning, a temporary ban (1 week to 6 months), or a permanent ban. you get one appeal if new context turns up.

adapted from the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
